The mini-LED is an
evolutionary technology
for LCD TVs

Measuring around 0.1 millimeter, mini-LEDs are less intricate than micro-LEDs(0.01 millimiter)
but are much more affordable and easiest to manufacture in bulk. As a transitional technology
for manufacutres. of LCD TVs, mini-LEDs have already seen traction in the market.
